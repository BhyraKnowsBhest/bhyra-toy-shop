### 1. SCOPE OF WORK & PRODUCTION
The Manufacturer agrees to produce, finish, package, and deliver the apparel styles specified in the Design Company’s official Tech Packs (including `GLOBAL_MASTER_TECH_PACK.md` and `SLIM_FIT_TECH_PACK.md`), subject to the terms of this Agreement.

### 2. QUALITY CONTROL & ACCEPTEBILITY STANDARDS (QA)
*   **Measurement Tolerance:** All finished garments must match the flat measurements specified in the Tech Pack within a tolerance threshold of +/- 0.5 cm for knits and +/- 0.3 cm for woven items. Any units falling outside this window fail inspection.
*   **Defect Allowance:** The acceptable quality limit (AQL) for structural defects (such as skipped stitches, loose threads, uneven hems, or misaligned pockets) is set at 1.5% of the total batch size. If defects exceed 1.5%, the Manufacturer must repair or replace the defective units at their own expense.

### 3. DELIVERY TIMELINES & PENALTIES
*   **Prototype Window:** The Manufacturer must deliver initial physical fit samples within 14 business days of receiving raw fabrics and digital pattern specs.
*   **Mass Production Delivery:** Bulk production orders must be completed and loaded for shipping freight within 45 calendar days from the date of initial down-payment clearance.
*   **Late Delivery Clause:** If delivery is delayed past the agreed completion date due to manufacturer fault, a penalty of 1% of the total invoice value will be deducted for every day delayed, capped at a maximum deduction of 15%.

### 4. PAYMENT TERMS & DEPOSIT STRUCTURE
*   **Payment Flow:** Production financing will follow a strict **50/50** milestone protocol. 
*   **Upfront Deposit:** 50% of the total calculated production invoice must be paid before the factory starts cutting bulk fabric.
*   **Final Balance:** The remaining 50% balance will be paid immediately upon successful third-party QA inspection and before the shipment is released from the factory warehouse floor.

### 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY PROTECTION
All patterns, vector artwork layouts, custom grading specifications, tech packs, and code blocks provided to the Manufacturer remain the sole intellectual property of the Design Company. The Manufacturer shall not reproduce, copy, or sell these custom designs to any third party.
